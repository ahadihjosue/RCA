Admission Policy

To consider the appropriateness of the education provided by our school for each student. We select students who scored higher grades in Mathematics, Physics and English each year.

The key criteria in selecting students started in November 2018 and was based on best score marks and passion to study coding at an early age, according to officials in Ministry of information and Communication Technology.

We welcome visitors throughout the academic year. A visit to RCA allows you to actually experience what the school is all about. Visits are relaxed and involve both meeting with the administration and visiting the classrooms and grounds.

Note: Applying for Rwanda Coding Academy starts each year after completion of Ordinary Level Examinations. When you get selected you will receive a call from the school for further information
